# Drawing, Graphics, and Effects

## Preparation Paths

### Rapid Review

This topic is optional for most interviews. Study
[Shapes, Paths, and Canvas](shapes-paths-and-canvas/README.md) when the role mentions
custom drawing, charts, or graphics-heavy interfaces.

### Standard Preparation

1. [Shapes, Paths, and Canvas](shapes-paths-and-canvas/README.md)
2. [Materials, Shaders, and Visual Effects](materials-shaders-and-visual-effects/README.md)
3. [Image Rendering and Export](image-rendering-and-export/README.md)

### Role-Specific Depth

For graphics, creative-tool, data-visualization, or design-system roles, rehearse all
three concepts with emphasis on performance measurement and accessibility fallbacks.

## Concepts

| Concept | Why it matters | Priority | Time |
|---|---|---|---:|
| [Shapes, Paths, and Canvas](shapes-paths-and-canvas/README.md) | Chooses retained or immediate drawing for custom visuals. | Situational | 6 min |
| [Materials, Shaders, and Visual Effects](materials-shaders-and-visual-effects/README.md) | Adds advanced effects with explicit performance awareness. | Situational | 6 min |
| [Image Rendering and Export](image-rendering-and-export/README.md) | Produces images from SwiftUI content for external use. | Situational | 6 min |
